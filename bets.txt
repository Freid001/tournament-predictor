=== FIFA 2026 World Cup ===
Bet       	= A placed bet.
Hedge     	= Opposes a placed bet.
Weak Candidate  = ELO >=40% AND Profit >£5 AND Profit <£10

Risky Candidate = ELO >=30% AND ELO <40% AND Profit >=£10
Moonshot        = ELO <30% AND Profit >=£20
Threat          = A bet or candidate whose success causes one or more existing bets to lose.
KO		= Knocks out a team to reach position.
